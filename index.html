Privacy Policy

Cine collects only the content that users explicitly provide (images, videos, music from device gallery). Minimal technical info (app version, device type) is collected for analytics.

To allow users to create and share reels within Cine, improve app performance, and anonymously analyze usage.

All content uploaded or shared by users remains their responsibility. Users should ensure any music or media added is not copyrighted or they have permission. Violating content may be removed.
